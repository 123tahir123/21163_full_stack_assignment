Chhichhore

Year: 2019

Duration: 2h 23m

Genre: Comedy/Drama

Director: Nitesh Tiwari

Plot: A tragic incident forces Anirudh, a middle-aged man, to take a trip down memory lane and reminisce his college days along with his friends, who were labelled as losers.
